From Digital Ticks to Emergent Forces: A Cascade of Resonant Steps

Abstract

We propose a discrete-time framework in which nature updates every ≈ 0.6 ps. That invariant “tick” (≈ 1.667 THz, 6.89 meV) seeds a resonant energy ladder whose per-step amplifier is \(Q \simeq 409.78\). In five steps the ladder lands on the electroweak scale (~ 80 GeV) while step 4 coincides with the QCD confinement scale (~ 195 MeV). Continuing to steps 6–8 projects ~ 32.6 TeV, 13.4 PeV, and 5.47 EeV, hinting that the entire hierarchy of forces is an arithmetic consequence of a single digital tick.

1. Introduction

The origin of the strong/weak hierarchy remains a central puzzle in high-energy physics. Instead of continuous renormalisation, we consider a digital-tick model: the universe advances in equal time quanta. Earlier work showed that a seven-segment phase-locking condition fixes geometrical constants; here we extend that logic to energy-scale emergence.

A 0.6 ps tick implies a base frequency 1.667 THz and, via \(E = h f\), a base quantum \(E_0 \approx 6.89\;\text{meV}\). We ask: what fixed multiplier \(Q\) maps that seed to the electroweak scale after precisely five resonant steps?

2. Deriving the Step Multiplier

Requiring step 5 to reach \(E_5 \approx 80\;\text{GeV}\) gives

\[ Q^5 \;=\; \frac{E_5}{E_0}. \]

\[ \frac{80\times10^{9}\;\text{eV}}{6.89\times10^{-3}\;\text{eV}} \;\approx\; 1.158\times10^{13}, \]

\[ Q \;\approx\; \bigl(1.158\times10^{13}\bigr)^{1/5} \;\approx\; 409.78. \]

Thus each rung multiplies energy by \(Q \approx 4.10\times10^{2}\). The resulting ladder is:

Step Energy Remarks

1 6.89 meV × 409.78 ≈ 2.83 eV Atomic scale
2 2.83 eV × 409.78 ≈ 1.16 keV X-ray scale
3 1.16 keV × 409.78 ≈ 0.475 MeV Sub-MeV
4 0.475 MeV × 409.78 ≈ 194.7 MeV Strong (QCD)
5 194.7 MeV × 409.78 ≈ 79.8 GeV Electroweak

3. Forces from the Ladder

Step 4 reproduces the canonical QCD scale where hadronic confinement sets in; the very next rung lands on the W/Z-boson mass window. In this picture the weak interaction emerges directly from the strong-force plateau rather than from an unrelated high-energy cut-off.

4. Beyond the Electroweak Step

Applying the same \(Q\) further:

  • Step 7: 32.6 TeV × 409.78 ≈ 13.4 PeV
  • Step 8: 13.4 PeV × 409.78 ≈ 5.47 EeV

The PeV-to-EeV domain overlaps observed knees in cosmic-ray spectra, suggesting that the same tick-driven resonance may govern both particle and astrophysical phenomena.

5. Discussion

A single multiplier \(Q\) reproduces the strong and electroweak scales and projects smoothly into the ultra-high-energy realm. Because \(Q\) is determined once the electroweak target is fixed, the model avoids arbitrary fine-tuning. The digital-tick paradigm therefore offers a compact alternative to traditional renormalisation-group flow.

6. Conclusions

Starting from a 0.6 ps tick (6.89 meV) and an empirically anchored multiplier \(Q \approx 409.78\), a five-step resonant cascade lands on 79.8 GeV, with the strong-force scale appearing one rung below. Extending the same factor yields natural predictions in the TeV-to-EeV range. Ongoing work will derive \(Q\) from first-principles lattice geometry and seek observational signatures of discrete-tick harmonics.
