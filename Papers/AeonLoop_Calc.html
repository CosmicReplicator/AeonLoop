\(S_{D_{\text{eff}}} = \frac{2\pi^{(D_{\text{eff}}/2)}}{\Gamma(D_{\text{eff}}/2)}\)

\(L_{\text{curv}} \sim \left(\frac{\hbar c}{\rho_{\text{vac}}^{(3D)}}\right)^{\frac{1}{4}}\)

\(\Lambda \sim \frac{1}{L_{\text{curv}}^2}\)

\(\exp[-\beta\cdot 0.667] \approx 0.0073\)

\(-\beta \cdot 0.667 \approx \ln(0.0073) \approx -4.922\)

\(\beta \approx \frac{4.922}{0.667} \approx 7.38\)

This demonstrates that the problematic fine‑tuning of \(\alpha\) is eliminated by the natural digital‑tick dynamics.
