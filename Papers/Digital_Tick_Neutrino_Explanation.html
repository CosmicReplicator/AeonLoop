right-handed neutrinos in certain Beyond Standard Model scenarios. The right-handed neutrino,

only very weakly.

acts as a natural, built-in scale. This not only underpins the relation:

ℏ · c ∼ E · L

but it also provides a self-contained explanation for why the right-handed neutrino, long sought after

in particle physics, might be nothing more than the signature of the error-correcting tick that governs

This connection illustrates the power of the digital-tick approach---it links discrete time, error correction,
