Introduction

  4. Digital Error Correction:
    The discrete digital tick stabilizes the phase alignment of trapped energy, ensuring that deviations are corrected in each successive tick. This built‑in error-correction further enhances the void's ability to serve as a seedbed for future cosmic structures.

Conclusion

Cosmic voids, traditionally viewed as empty spaces, may actually provide optimal conditions for trapping light and energy with perfect phase coherence. This process creates a precondition where, upon the arrival of matter, rapid formation of galaxies or massive black holes becomes possible. In this way, cosmic voids act not as direct birthplaces but as the perfect seedbeds for cosmic structure formation.
